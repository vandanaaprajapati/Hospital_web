24/7 Emergency Service

The Millennium Multispeciality Hospital And Diagnostic Center has a full fledged 24/7 casualty services department that attends to any emergency. Equipped to cope up with emergency of any kind, the department has the state of the art acute medical care unit to handle emergencies round the clock and presence of surgeons to handle the trauma cases.

Extensive OT

We have Operation Theaters equipped with all the modern facilities & we ensure a strict hygiene control for the safety of our patients as per the standard protocols.

Insurance & TPA Partners

The Millennium Multispeciality Hospital And Diagnostic Center is tied up with many Insurance & Third Party Administrators (TPA) for hassle free cashless medical facilities.

ICU & NICU Services

The Millennium Multispeciality Hospital And Diagnostic Center, is Equipped with all state of the art, equipment such as multi -para monitors, defibrillators, ventilator, Centralized 02, Compressed air with Remote Control, Post-Operative-care of critically ill Patient of Prolonged & Complications-such as neurosurgery, Urosugery, Plastic, Orthopedic & Other Surgeries.
